Health Education for Everyone

ProstateAdvice is designed to be usable by all people. We follow WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and continuously improve our accessibility.

High Contrast

Our color system meets WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratios. Enable high contrast mode below for even stronger visibility, or use our dark mode for reduced eye strain.

All animations and transitions respect the prefers-reduced-motion media query. If your OS is set to reduce motion, our site automatically disables all animations.

Content is written at an accessible reading level. Medical terms are explained plainly. We avoid jargon and use strength-focused, positive language throughout.

Every page is designed mobile-first and tested across phones, tablets, and desktops. Touch targets are at least 44px for comfortable tapping. Layouts adapt fluidly.
