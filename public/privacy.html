Technical Details

How Your Data Works

A transparent look at the browser technologies we use to keep your data private.

localStorage

Simple preferences like your language, theme, and disclaimer acceptance are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

IndexedDB

Structured data like assessment scores, training check-ins, and history are stored in IndexedDB, a browser database. It supports larger datasets while staying completely local.

Zero Server Calls

No data is transmitted to any server. The site is static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Your browser does all the processing locally.
