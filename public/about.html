Who We Are

A Proactive Health Education Platform

ProstateAdvice is a free, open educational platform built for men who want to take early ownership of their prostate health. We combine interactive self-assessment tools, guided pelvic training, and evidence-graded supplement reviews into a single, privacy-first experience.

Our audience is men aged 18–40 who already invest in fitness, nutrition, and mental health—but have never been given the tools to proactively strengthen their prostate. We treat prostate health as a performance domain, not a medical scare.

Our Principles

What We Believe

Seven principles that guide everything we create.

Strength-Focused

We frame prostate health as building and maintaining strength, not avoiding disease. Proactive, not reactive.

Performance-Oriented

We speak the language of optimization, training, and measurable progress that resonates with active, health-conscious men.

Science-Grounded

Every claim is backed by peer-reviewed research. We grade evidence transparently so you can make informed decisions.

Zero Fear Tactics

No scare headlines, no guilt-based marketing, no worst-case-scenario framing. Empowerment over anxiety.

Masculinity Without Toxicity

We respect masculine identity and self-improvement culture while promoting openness, honesty, and vulnerability about health.

No Supplement Hype

We never sell supplements. Our reviews are evidence-graded and free of affiliate bias or miracle claims.

Privacy-Forward

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking cookies, no selling of intimate health data. Export or delete anytime.
